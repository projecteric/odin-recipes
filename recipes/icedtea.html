The REAL Long Island Iced Tea

Back to recipe index

Description:

The only acceptable way to cool down on a hot day! Cheers!

Reference recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2oz vodka
  • 1/2oz rum
  • 1/2oz gin
  • 1/2oz tequila
  • 1/2oz triple sec
  • 1oz sweet and sour mix
  • 1oz cola
  • 1 lemon slice

Steps

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Pour vodka, rum, gin, tequila, triple sec, and sour mix over ice; cover and shake
  3. Pour cocktail into a Collins or hurricane glass; top with splash of cola for color.
  4. Garnish with a lemon slice.
